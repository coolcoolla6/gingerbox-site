Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

GingerBox is designed as a local-first recipe and ingredient management app. Most app data stays on your device unless you choose to use optional features such as Google Drive backup.

Overview

GingerBox is a recipe and ingredient management application that helps you save recipes, manage ingredients, track expiry dates, and organize shopping lists.

The app does not require account registration. Most data is stored locally on your device.

Information We Collect

GingerBox does not require you to create an account or provide personal profile information to use the main app features.

If you choose to enable analytics, anonymous usage information may be collected through Firebase Analytics to help us understand how the app is used and improve the user experience.

Diagnostic crash information may be collected through Firebase Crashlytics to help improve app stability and fix errors.

Recipe, Ingredient, and Shopping Data

Recipe data, ingredient data, shopping lists, and app settings are stored locally on your device unless you choose to back them up to Google Drive.

GingerBox does not operate its own server for storing your recipe or ingredient data.

Google Drive Backup and Restore

GingerBox provides optional backup and restore features using Google Drive. Backup data is uploaded only when you explicitly start a backup.

Restore data is accessed only when you choose to restore a backup from Google Drive.

Third-Party Services

GingerBox may use the following third-party services to provide optional features, analytics, diagnostics, and backup functionality:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Drive API
  • Google Firebase Remote Config

Data Control

Because most GingerBox data is stored on your device, deleting the app may also delete locally stored app data from that device.

If you created a Google Drive backup, you can manage or delete the backup file from your Google Drive account.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or GingerBox privacy practices, please contact us.
